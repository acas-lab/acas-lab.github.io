Accelerator System Design

Our work in Accelerator System Design spans AI Chip Design, domain-specific optimization, hardware/software co-design, and on-device AI acceleration. We're driven to achieve peak performance by seamlessly integrating hardware and software, crafting tailored hardware solutions for specific applications. A major challenge we tackle is the escalating size and complexity of large-scale AI models (LLMs), for which we're pioneering optimized system architectures to ensure their effective and efficient deployment.
